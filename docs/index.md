<h1>como</h1>

<p>
  Como (for <i>collective movement</i>) is a framework to develop web applications for gesture/movement interaction with sounds. It is applies in artistic creation and pedagogy, workshops, as well as to education and rehabition. Specific applications are derived from the generic framework, such as CoMo Education, Como-Rehabiliation or CoMo-Vox (in collaboration with Radio France, supported by the Ministere of Education).
